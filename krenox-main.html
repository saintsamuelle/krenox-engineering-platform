About Krenox

Krenox is a 17-member tech and engineering team driven by a passion for aerospace innovation, software development, systems architecture, and AI. From designing NACA airfoil wing models and building ESP32-powered smart systems to developing sustainability apps and engineering collaboration platforms, our team spans aerodynamics, avionics, embedded systems, UI/UX design, web development, and quality assurance. We are united by one goal: to advance technology and serve our community with excellence.
